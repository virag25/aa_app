1.1 Aadarsh Amdavad is a voluntary social organization sponsored by H.S.S.S.S. Charitable Trust. Aadarsh Amdavad started activities from January 2004.

1.2 The Trust is registered as a Public Charitable Trust on 11th March, 2002, bearing       (1) Registration No. E/15232/Ahmedabad.(2)  Pan No. AAATH2354N (3) 80 G U/S-Approval for Income Tax Relief on Donation (4) FCRA Permission to get Foreign Donation

Objects of the Trust:

1. Medical Relief

2. Relief to the poor

3. Educational Objects

4. Other charitable objects: Such as promotion of social and cultural activities and   general public utility on a non profit basis irrespective of caste, creed, community or gender orientation.

(1) Convener: Shri Bharatbhai Shah

(2) General Manager: Shri Kirit Shah-Looking after Administrative Work

(3) Administative Staff:

Coordinator(Yog) Shri Snehal Mashruwala is a voluntary activist. He looks after training aspects of all the yog related activities.

Administrative staff includes S/Shri Harish Malavania, Dilip Gandhi, Ghanshyam Acharya, Devendrabhai Soni and Laxmiben Solanki.

The hall marks of the administration of ‘Aadarsh Amdavad’ are simplicity, economy and transparency.
